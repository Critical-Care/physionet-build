Submission Statuses
  1. Submitted - Awaiting editor assignment or decision.
  2. Rejected - Decision 1. The submission ends.
  3. Resubmission - Decision 2. Awaiting submitting author to make revisions and resubmit.
  4. Accepted/Copyedit - Decision 3. The editor accepts the submission, and makes small changes to the metadata and/or files. Authors must approve changes before editor completes this step.
  5. Awaiting Publication
