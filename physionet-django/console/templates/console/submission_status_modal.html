Submission Statuses
  1. Submitted - Awaiting editor assignment or decision.
  2. Rejected - editor decision 1. The submission ends.
  3. Resubmission - editor decision 2. Awaiting submitting author to make revisions and resubmit.
  4. Accepted/Copyedit - editor decision 3. The editor accepts the submission, and makes small changes to the metadata and/or files.
  5. Copyedit complete. Awaiting approval from authors to publish.
  6. All authors approved. Awaiting editor to publish.
